Introduction to ParaSwap

ParaSwap is a leading decentralized exchange (DEX) aggregator that helps users achieve the best possible prices for token swaps across multiple blockchains. By aggregating liquidity from top DEXs like Uniswap, SushiSwap, Curve, Balancer, and others, ParaSwap routes your trades through the most efficient paths with minimal slippage and fees. Its fast-growing ecosystem is trusted by thousands of DeFi users for cost-effective, transparent, and secure swaps.

What is ParaSwap?

ParaSwap is built to optimize every trade by splitting orders across different decentralized exchanges and liquidity sources. It uses advanced algorithms to find the most favorable rates and provides detailed analytics on price impact and fees. ParaSwap supports major blockchains including Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Avalanche, and Optimism. Users can swap, bridge assets, and even place limit orders directly from a single interface—without giving up custody of their funds.

  • Aggregates liquidity from 20+ major DEXs

  • Non-custodial swaps — users control their funds at all times

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Use ParaSwap

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask, WalletConnect, Ledger, or other supported wallets.
  2. Select Blockchain: Choose the chain you want to swap on (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.).
  3. Select Tokens: Pick the token you want to sell and the token you wish to receive.
  4. Review Best Prices: ParaSwap displays routes and expected fees before you swap.
  5. Confirm and Execute: Approve in your wallet, sign the transaction, and track it in real time.
  6. Advanced: Use limit orders or private swap features if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – ParaSwap

Is ParaSwap Safe?

Yes, ParaSwap uses audited smart contracts and never takes custody of your funds. Always check you are on the official website.

Which Blockchains Does ParaSwap Support?

ParaSwap works on Ethereum, Polygon, BNB Chain, Avalanche, Optimism, and more.

ParaSwap charges low swap fees, always shown before you approve a transaction. Network gas fees also apply.

Does ParaSwap Support Limit Orders?

Yes. You can set custom buy or sell prices and ParaSwap will execute your order once market conditions are met.

Can I Use Hardware Wallets?

Yes, you can connect Ledger, Trezor, and other hardware wallets for maximum security.

Where Can I Track My Swaps?

All swap activity is visible in your connected wallet and the ParaSwap dashboard.
